- invoice reference
- job reference
- other operational invoice identifiers needed for later back-costing and reconciliation
3. Xero push exists, but SteelIQ does not yet clearly show when the invoice has been paid / part-paid / remains unpaid based on Xero feedback.
4. The workflow needs to become more operationally complete:
- accepted quote
- create project
- create job
- create invoice
- invoice linked to the job and carrying meaningful reference into SteelIQ and Xero
