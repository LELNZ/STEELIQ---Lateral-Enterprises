CURRENT PROBLEM TO FIX

Current grouped/custom quote-builder logic effectively assumes one shared height across all panes/segments in the grouped layout.

This fails for real-world designs such as:
- a three-pane grouped window where the center pane is taller than the two side panes

Example target case:
- overall width: 1950
- overall height: 1320
- left pane height: 1010
- centre pane height: 1320
- right pane height: 1010

The builder must support individual pane heights, not just one common height for all panes.
